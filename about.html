About Wilma Nachsin

Wilma Nachsin helps clients in transition with career strategies, resumes, and career and life coaching services. She has helped more than 300 clients realize their job and career goals. Her resumes and job search strategies get results. For example, one of her clients was selected for interviews out of 1600 applicants, dazzled the hiring manager, and got the job. Wilma leads job strategy and support groups, is a frequent speaker on diverse career topics, and earned her Masters and Bachelors degrees as well as numerous certifications in career assessments, resume writing, coaching, mediation, and other related areas. She works with Arlene Wanetick on many initiatives, including the Tandem Coach Team, providing unique value to clients by offering a special blend of insights gleaned from decades of combined experiences in advertising, creativity, art, human resources, counseling, resume writing, and coaching.


Wilma Nachsin, MS, CCC, PHR, Career and Life Coach, is a job strategist and possibility creator who has made a career of teaching, training, development, and coaching. She inspires and guides groups, individuals, and workshop participants through the job transition process.
